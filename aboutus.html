ABOUT US

"HIRE"

Founded by Prakhar Gupta and in 2017, HIRE is an platform that is building a futuristic online source to provide a highly immersive and interactive learning path to millions of students and enable them to tap into new-age tech opportunities. HIRE has placed over 7,500 aspirants in over 700 companies including Google, Lenskart, Razorpay, Flipkart, Zomato, Deloitte, Meesho etc.



In India, more than a million college graduates fail to get relevant employment opportunities every year, and yet there are thousands of companies struggling to find talent. HIRE's learning platform aims to bridge this gap.
